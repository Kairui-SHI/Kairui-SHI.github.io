Research

At the broadest level, my research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision, photogrammetry and machine learning.

Specifically, I'm interested in 3D reconstruction, focusing on high-precision mapping, reconstruction, and restoration of objects, streets, and cities, while also perceiving embedded information to enable interaction.

So, are we able to create an immersive and interactive virtual world? With the advancement of neural networks, hardware performance, and robotics, the speed and accuracy of reconstruction will continue to improve. This is not a research direction limited to a single field; its impact will inevitably extend to multiple domains, such as medicine, anthropology, and the gaming industry—seemingly unrelated fields or industries.
